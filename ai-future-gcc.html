Shaping the Future of the GCC

Executive Summary

Artificial Intelligence is one of the most powerful technologies of the modern age. It allows machines to learn, analyze information, solve problems and support human decision-making. For the GCC, AI is not only a technology trend — it is a strategic tool for economic diversification, smart governance and innovation-led growth.

GCC Perspective

GCC countries are investing in AI strategies, smart cities, digital government, advanced education and research institutions. AI can help the region improve public services, develop new industries, support sustainability and strengthen global competitiveness.

The Future of AI in the GCC

The future of AI in the GCC will depend on talent, data, regulation, infrastructure and responsible innovation. Countries that combine AI with education, entrepreneurship and strong digital infrastructure will lead the next phase of regional transformation.
