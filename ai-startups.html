Building the Next Generation of Intelligent Businesses

Artificial Intelligence startups are transforming industries through automation, machine learning, computer vision, robotics, and intelligent software, creating new opportunities across the GCC.

Overview

AI startups develop products and services powered by artificial intelligence. These companies work across healthcare, finance, education, transportation, manufacturing, cybersecurity, and many other industries. They are becoming a key driver of innovation and economic diversification.

Major AI Startup Sectors

🤖 Machine Learning

Developing systems that learn from data to improve predictions and decision-making.

👁 Computer Vision

AI that interprets images and videos for healthcare, security and smart cities.

🏭 AI Industry

Automation, predictive maintenance and intelligent manufacturing systems.

Future of AI Startups in the GCC

With strong government support, investment, and digital transformation, the GCC is becoming an attractive destination for AI entrepreneurs. AI startups will contribute to innovation, job creation, and the development of knowledge-based economies.
