Blockchain

Creating Trust in the Digital Economy

Blockchain is a decentralized digital ledger that securely records transactions, enhances transparency, reduces fraud, and enables trusted digital ecosystems across governments, businesses and financial institutions.

Executive Summary

Blockchain technology enables secure, transparent and tamper-resistant record keeping. Its applications extend beyond cryptocurrencies into finance, government, healthcare, supply chains, digital identity and smart contracts, making it a key technology for the GCC's digital future.

"Blockchain creates trust where trust was previously difficult to establish."

The Future of Blockchain

Blockchain will increasingly support digital identity, cross-border trade, smart government, financial innovation, and secure data sharing. Combined with Artificial Intelligence, IoT and cloud computing, it will become a foundational technology for future digital economies.
