Climate Change

Understanding the global climate crisis and how the GCC is leading solutions for a sustainable future.

🌍 What is Climate Change?

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in temperature and weather patterns caused mainly by human activities like burning fossil fuels and deforestation.

🔥 Main Causes

• Greenhouse gas emissions
• Fossil fuel burning
• Industrial pollution
• Deforestation
• Overconsumption of energy

🌡️ Effects on the World

Rising temperatures, melting ice caps, extreme weather, droughts, floods, and rising sea levels are some major effects impacting ecosystems and human life.

🏜️ Impact on GCC

The GCC region faces rising heat, water scarcity, desertification, and pressure on infrastructure — pushing governments to invest in sustainability.

🌱 GCC Response

GCC countries are investing in renewable energy, carbon reduction projects, smart cities, and environmental protection initiatives to fight climate change.
