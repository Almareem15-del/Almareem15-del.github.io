Cybersecurity in the GCC

As GCC countries expand digital government, AI, smart cities and cloud computing, cybersecurity has become a national priority. Strong cyber resilience helps protect economic growth, public services and critical infrastructure while building trust in digital transformation.

"Cybersecurity is no longer only an IT function—it is a foundation of national resilience, economic stability and digital trust."

The Future of Cybersecurity

Future cybersecurity will increasingly rely on Artificial Intelligence, Zero Trust architectures, quantum-resistant encryption, advanced identity management and international cooperation to defend digital societies against increasingly sophisticated threats.
