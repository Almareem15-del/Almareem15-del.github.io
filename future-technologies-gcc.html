Future Technologies and the GCC

Across the GCC, national development strategies increasingly emphasize research, innovation, AI, advanced manufacturing, space science, clean energy and entrepreneurship. These investments aim to build diversified, knowledge-driven economies capable of competing globally.

Looking Ahead

The next decade will be defined by technological convergence— where Artificial Intelligence, robotics, cloud computing, blockchain, IoT, quantum technologies and biotechnology work together to create smarter, more sustainable and more resilient societies across the GCC and beyond.
