Internet of Things

Connecting Devices, Data and Intelligent Cities

The Internet of Things (IoT) connects physical devices through the internet, allowing them to collect, exchange and analyze data in real time to improve efficiency, automation and decision-making.

Executive Summary

IoT has become a foundation of digital transformation. From smart homes and connected factories to intelligent transportation and healthcare, IoT enables billions of devices to communicate and create valuable real-time insights.
