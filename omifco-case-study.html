OVERVIEW

Oman India Fertiliser Company, known as OMIFCO, is one of Oman’s important industrial organizations and a major contributor to the fertilizer sector.

Located in Sur, Oman, OMIFCO plays a role in supporting agriculture, industrial development, and economic cooperation while moving toward more responsible and sustainable operations.
